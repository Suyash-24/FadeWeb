Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about setting up and dominating with Fade.

How do I invite Fade to my server?
Simply click the "Add to Discord" button in the top right corner of the website. You will be redirected to Discord's official authorization page where you can select the server you want to invite Fade to. Make sure you have the "Manage Server" permission!
Is Fade completely free to use?
Yes! All of Fade's core features—including Moderation, AutoMod, Logging, and the Economy system—are 100% free forever. We do offer a premium tier for communities that want ultra-customizable aesthetics (like fully custom welcome cards) and higher rate limits.
How does the global economy system work?
The economy is global across all servers running Fade. Users can claim daily rewards, gamble their balance, and climb the global leaderboards. Server admins can also create local items in a custom shop that users can buy with their global balance.
Can I customize the welcome cards?
Absolutely. Fade's welcome card engine is best-in-class. You can customize the background image, the gradient colors, the font style, and what text is displayed (like member count or username). You can configure all of this directly through our interactive setup command.
How do I setup AutoMod and Anti-Raid?
Our Anti-Raid and AutoMod modules are incredibly powerful but simple to setup. Use the /setup automod command to open the interactive dashboard right inside Discord. From there, you can toggle on link filtering, spam protection, and automated kick/ban thresholds with one click.
